About This Project

The Garment Payroll System is designed to revolutionize how small to medium-sized garment factories manage their workforce compensation. Our system addresses the unique challenges of the garment industry where workers may be paid by the hour, by the piece, or through a combination of both.

Key problems we solve:

  • Complex pay structures with multiple calculation methods
  • Time-consuming manual payroll calculations
  • Difficulty tracking piece-rate work accurately
  • Lack of transparency in payroll processing
  • Challenges in generating compliance reports

This system was developed after extensive research with garment factory owners to create a solution that truly meets their needs.

Flexible Pay Calculations

Supports hourly wages, piece-rate payments, overtime, and bonus calculations with customizable rules.

Mobile-Friendly

Accessible on any device, allowing supervisors to record work on the factory floor.

Data Security

Role-based access control ensures sensitive payroll data is protected.

Comprehensive Reporting

Generate reports for management, workers, and compliance requirements with ease.
